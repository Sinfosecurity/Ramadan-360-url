FAQ

  • Prayer times not loading? The app falls back to offline calculation. Ensure location is allowed, or set your city manually. If offline, wait a moment for the offline calculation to appear.
  • Audio not playing? First play while online to cache a surah; afterwards it works offline. Keep the app updated for the latest fixes.
  • Bookmarks or downloads? All Quran text and audio are stored locally on your device; clearing app data removes them.
